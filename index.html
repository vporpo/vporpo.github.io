v(LastName)google.com

I am a compiler developer at Google in the Bay Area, developing a new auto-vectorizer in upstream LLVM based on Sandbox IR.

Before joining Google I was a compiler developer at Intel in Santa Clara, and before that I was workng on compilers at the University of Cambridge.

I did my Ph.D. in compilers at the School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh. My thesis was on code generation for scalable low-power VLIW processors in GCC.
